FirstSwim teaches toddlers and kids to swim with confidence and safety.

FirstSwim wants children to not only be safe in the water, but to enjoy it as well.

Swimming is an incredible activity for people of all ages and learning the skills and confidence at an early age enables both the child and parents to enjoy this activity together.

Lessons are conducted Monday–Friday in 30 minute sessions. Each lesson is done one–on–one with the child and instructor. On Friday lessons are concluded with a Parent/Grandparent/Guardian lesson in which the instructor teaches the Parent how to continue the lessons with the children to provide the optimal chance of success.

FAQ

How long is the lesson program?
Each session is 30 minutes, for five consecutive days, Monday through Friday.
What ages does FirstSwim work with?
Children that are 2+ years old.
Can I stay and watch during a lesson?
Parents are asked not to be seen by the children for the first few days of lessons, but they are more than welcome to watch from a spot that the child can not see them. Also, for the Friday lesson parents are encouraged to get in the pool with the instructor and child to learn how to continue working the skills that were taught.
What happens in the event of a weather cancellation?
If one day is missed, the lesson can be made up at a different time. However, if more than one is missed the rest of the week's lessons are cancelled and we'll have to schedule in the child for a different week.
How is FirstSwim different than other types of infant or toddler swimming lessons?
Safety and prevention is always the top priority for children in and around the pool. FirstSwim goes beyond just accident prevention and works to build a child's confidence in the water so they can have fun and become strong swimmers. Combining awareness, confidence, and skills helps prepare children to react to accidents in a calm and practiced way, and let's everyone enjoy the water with more peace of mind.
